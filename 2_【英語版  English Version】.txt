• Type B: Scavenger

An even more surprising event occurred. When Type C exceeded a certain number of predations, it "sublimated" and transformed into a massive, long-lived individual: "Type E (Elder)." Type E fertilizes its surroundings, accelerating the chain of life further.
And when these individuals clustered together, we observed signs of "Type F (Collective Intelligence)," something that transcends our understanding.

This is not a simulation.
Right now, before our very eyes, a "seed of intelligence" from the abyss of the universe is about to sprout.
